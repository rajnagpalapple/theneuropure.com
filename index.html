Coridalis

Passiflora

**GABA (from Passionflower)**: GABA, or gamma-aminobutyric acid, is a neurotransmitter that inhibits nerve activity. Derived from Passionflower in the context of NeuroPure, GABA has a soothing effect on nerves, potentially reducing pain and discomfort.
